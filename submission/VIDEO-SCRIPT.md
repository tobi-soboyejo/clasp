# Demo video — shot-by-shot (target ≤ 2:55)

**Prep before recording:** browser at ~110% zoom, 1080p+ screen recording
(QuickTime is fine), second browser profile or phone for the "client" role,
MetaMask public name set (Lookup → My wallet → Set public name), one full
rehearsal with a throwaway agreement.

## 0:00–0:25 · The story (voiceover over claspscore.com landing)
"Last year a client owed me $2,000 for completed work and disappeared. I'm
still chasing it legally. There was no way to check his payment history
before I took the job — and no way for the next freelancer to learn from
what happened to me. So I built the credit check I wish I'd run. This is
Clasp."

## 0:25–1:05 · The handshake (screen recording)
- New agreement → fill live: client address, $500, deadline a few minutes
  out (so it expires on camera), one-line scope → MetaMask sign → shareable
  link appears.
- Open the co-sign link AS THE CLIENT (phone / second profile) → tap
  **"Sign for free (no gas)"**:
  "My client doesn't own crypto and doesn't need any — they sign like it's
  DocuSign."
- Paste the signature code back as yourself → submit → badge flips ACTIVE.

## 1:05–1:50 · The record (the drama beat)
- Countdown hits zero, turns red → **Flag default**:
  "That flag is now permanent, public history — no admin can delete it, no
  lawyer can threaten it away."

  → Bright Smile Dental, **419 · BAD**, red comet, "High risk of

  "The score isn't a black box — every input is printed. Same formula for

## 1:50–2:25 · The ecosystem
- Board page, scroll: "A job board where every poster wears their payment
  history." Point at the dental clinic's listing wearing its 419 chip.
- Click through to a track record: rehire stat.

## 2:25–2:55 · Why onchain + close (over the contract page on the explorer)
"Any web2 version of this gets sued into deleting records — the worst
clients are the most litigious. A contract on Monad has no one to send the
cease-and-desist to, and nobody appears in it without their own signature.
Three contracts, verified source, every number you saw read live from
chain. Clasp — the credit check for deals between strangers.
claspscore.com."
